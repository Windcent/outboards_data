Outboards Performance Viewer Guide

1 Search & Select Engines

Use the Search & Select Engines filter in the sidebar to search for engines. You can type in search queries and select engines from the dropdown to add them to your workspace. Use [Add All Filtered Engines] to quickly add everything matching your search criteria.

2 Refine with Filters

Narrow down your comparisons by choosing specific Horsepower (HP) levels, Engine Configurations (Single, Twin, etc.), or Hull Types. Use the dual-handle Boat Length slider to restrict boat lengths.

3 Performance Metrics

Toggle between three main metrics to display on the chart:

  • Fuel (GPH): Fuel consumption rate in gallons/liters per hour.
  • Economy (GPM / L/mi): Fuel economy rate per distance traveled.
  • Speed (MPH / knts / kph): Boat speed over ground.

Use the "Per Engine" toggle to divide fuel/economy consumption values by the engine count.

4 Unit Systems

Customize your viewing preference by changing units on the fly for **Speed** (MPH, knots, kph), **Length** (feet, meters), and **Volume** (gallons, liters). The interface handles conversion instantly.

5 Advanced Color Modes

Use the Color Mode dropdown under the chart to group or color datasets by boat manufacturer, exact boat length, length range, engine key, engine brand, hull type, or wind velocity.

6 Chart Controls & Export

  • Interactive Legend: Click on items in the Plotted Curves Legend or Color Group Legend to toggle visibility of individual lines or entire groups on the chart.
  • Zoom/Pan: Use your mouse wheel to zoom in/out, or click-and-drag to pan the graph. Click Reset Zoom to restore the default view.
  • Data Table: Click on any data point in the chart to automatically scroll to and highlight its details in the bottom table.
  • Export Actions: Export your current view using the PDF button (for printing or saving a static report) or the HTML button to download a standalone, fully-interactive dashboard containing the selected datasets.
